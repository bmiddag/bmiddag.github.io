About

Hi there, and welcome to my webpage! I am Bart Middag. I recently graduated from Ghent University as Master of Science in Computer Science Engineering. There, I was known for the extra mile I went for my projects to make them truly excellent and give them a creative touch whenever possible, both programming-wise and design-wise. As such, I am a developer who has acquired skills in various creative domains: a creative developer.

One domain that allows me to combine my creative skills is video game development. Since I was only 11 years old, I have been programming, writing, drawing and composing to develop my own video games. It is my passion, so I am very interested in putting these skills to use in a work environment, whether as a game programmer, a front-end developer or something else entirely.
